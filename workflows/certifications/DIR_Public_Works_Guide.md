# Guide 2: DIR Public Works Registration

## I. Overview
* **Purpose:** State compliance for public works, hauling, and facility maintenance tasks.
* **Requirement:** Required if performing work on state or local government properties.

## II. Execution Steps
1. **Navigate:** https://www.dir.ca.gov/Public-Works/PublicWorks.html
2. **Create Account:** Register as a Public Works Contractor with your EIN and business email.
3. **Specify Work:** Select classifications: Janitorial Services, Debris Removal, Facilities Maintenance, and Refuse Collection.
4. **Insurance Documentation:** Upload General Liability ($1,000,000 minimum) and Auto Liability policies naming the State of California as certificate holder.
5. **Fee:** Pay the annual fee ($300).
6. **Link Profile:** Link your approved DIR number to your Cal eProcure profile.

## III. Ongoing Maintenance
* Renew registration annually.
