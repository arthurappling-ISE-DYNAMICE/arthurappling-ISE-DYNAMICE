## THE 10-POINT INSPECTION

### POINT 1 — BEFORE PHOTOS (PRE-WORK DOCUMENTATION)
**Standard:** Every room and area in scope must be photographed before a single item is moved or cleaned.
- [ ] All rooms photographed (wide angle — full room visible)
- [ ] All damage, stains, and debris documented with close-up shots
- [ ] Exterior / entry photographed
- [ ] Photos timestamped and uploaded to: `vault/[CLIENT]/photos/before/`

**Pass:** Minimum 8 photos. Every area in scope covered.
**Fail:** Any area worked without a before photo — job is paused until documented.

### POINT 2 — DEBRIS AND HAUL-AWAY
**Standard:** All debris removed before cleaning begins. No hauling after paint.
- [ ] All furniture, appliances, and personal property removed (per scope)
- [ ] Debris staged for load-out — not left in hallways or on property
- [ ] Dump run completed — receipt obtained and photographed
- [ ] Disposal receipt uploaded to: `vault/[CLIENT]/receipts/`

**Pass:** Unit is clear of all debris. Disposal receipt on file.
**Fail:** Any debris remaining. Missing receipt = expense not billable.

### POINT 3 — DEEP CLEAN — KITCHEN
- [ ] All appliances cleaned inside and out (oven, fridge, microwave)
- [ ] Cabinets wiped inside and out — no grease or residue
- [ ] Countertops, sink, and faucets scrubbed and sanitized
- [ ] Floor mopped — no standing water left

**Pass:** Kitchen passes white glove test on all surfaces.
**Fail:** Any appliance interior not cleaned. Any grease residue on cabinets.

### POINT 4 — DEEP CLEAN — BATHROOMS
- [ ] Toilet sanitized inside, outside, and base
- [ ] Shower / tub scrubbed — no mildew, no soap scum
- [ ] Sink and mirror cleaned
- [ ] Floor scrubbed — grout lines included
- [ ] Exhaust fan wiped

**Pass:** No odor. No visible mold or mildew. All fixtures shine.
**Fail:** Any mildew remaining. Any odor present at inspection.

### POINT 5 — DEEP CLEAN — ALL ROOMS
- [ ] All walls wiped down (remove scuffs, dust, cobwebs)
- [ ] Baseboards wiped
- [ ] Window sills and blinds cleaned
- [ ] Closets emptied and cleaned
- [ ] Floors swept, vacuumed, and mopped

**Pass:** No dust, no debris, no odor in any room.
**Fail:** Any room not cleaned before painter enters.

### POINT 6 — PAINT PREP
- [ ] All holes patched and sanded smooth
- [ ] All scuffs and marks spot-primed
- [ ] Tape applied at trim, ceilings, and fixtures
- [ ] Floors protected with drop cloth or paper

**Pass:** All surfaces prepped. No bare patches or unprimed spots.
**Fail:** Paint applied over holes, cracks, or unprimed surfaces.

### POINT 7 — PAINT APPLICATION
- [ ] Two full coats applied to all walls
- [ ] Trim and doors painted or touched up per scope
- [ ] No roller marks, drips, or uneven coverage
- [ ] Color matches scope specification

**Pass:** Two coats. No drips. Consistent finish throughout.
**Fail:** Single coat. Visible roller marks. Missed trim.

### POINT 8 — FINAL CLEAN (POST-PAINT)
- [ ] Paint overspray wiped from floors, fixtures, and windows
- [ ] All tape and drop cloths removed and disposed
- [ ] Final vacuum / mop of all floors
- [ ] All windows cleaned

**Pass:** No paint on floors or fixtures. Unit is move-in ready.
**Fail:** Any paint residue on non-painted surfaces.

### POINT 9 — AFTER PHOTOS (POST-WORK DOCUMENTATION)
- [ ] All rooms re-photographed (same angles as before photos)
- [ ] Close-up of all repaired areas (patches, paint, fixtures)
- [ ] Exterior / entry re-photographed
- [ ] Photos timestamped and uploaded to: `vault/[CLIENT]/photos/after/`

**Pass:** Every before photo has a matching after photo.
**Fail:** Any area worked without a documented after photo — job cannot be closed.

### POINT 10 — FINAL WALKTHROUGH + SIGN-OFF
- [ ] Crew lead confirms all 9 prior points are PASS
- [ ] Prime Pathwy supervisor (or designated rep) walks the unit
- [ ] Any punch list items identified and corrected before sign-off
- [ ] Client (property manager / owner) walkthrough completed
- [ ] Written completion confirmation obtained (email or signed form)

**Pass:** All 10 points PASS. Written sign-off on file.
**Fail:** Any open punch list item. Verbal-only sign-off.
