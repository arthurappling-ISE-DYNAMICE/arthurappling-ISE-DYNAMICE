# Turnover Inspection Checklist — Summary
**Classification:** Foundational
**Category:** Workflow / SOP
**Owner:** Arthur F. Appling Sr. — AA Capital Inc. / Prime Pathwy
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-17

---

## Purpose

10-Point Subcontractor QC Standard for Prime Pathwy's property turnover revenue operation. Enforces Institutional Grade documentation on every turnover job: before photos, per-point pass/fail inspection, disposal receipts, after photos, and written sign-off. No verbal agreements. No undocumented work.

This is the legal and operational backbone of Revenue Engine #1.

---

## Practical Use Cases

- Full turnover (clean + haul + paint) — all 10 points apply
- Clean-out only — Points 1, 2, 5, 9, 10 mandatory; skip 6 and 7
- Haul-away only — Points 1, 2, 9, 10 mandatory; all others N/A
- Paint only — Points 1, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10 mandatory
- Any job type where liability dispute is possible — full 10-point audit regardless of scope

---

## Key Outputs

- Completed 10-Point Inspection Checklist with PASS/FAIL per point — stored in `vault/[CLIENT]/reports/`
- Before photo set — minimum 8 photos, timestamped — `vault/[CLIENT]/photos/before/`
- After photo set — matching before coverage — `vault/[CLIENT]/photos/after/`
- Disposal receipt (if haul-away) — `vault/[CLIENT]/receipts/`
- Written sign-off — crew lead + Prime Pathwy supervisor + client

---

## Dependencies

- Smartphone or camera capable of timestamped photos
- Cloud storage access for vault upload (Google Drive or equivalent)
- Prime Pathwy supervisor availability for Point 10 walkthrough
- Written completion form or client email confirmation capability

---

## DSCR Gate

**Estimated Output/Input Ratio:** High — foundational revenue protection, not a cost center.

The checklist eliminates dispute liability on every job. A single undocumented job that results in a dispute can cost 10–50x the time required to complete the checklist. Documentation time per job: 20–30 minutes. Risk mitigation value: protects the entire job margin plus future client relationship.
