# Sovereign Grant Acquisition Engine SOP — Risks & Limitations
**Owner:** Arthur F. Appling Sr. — AA Capital Inc. / Prime Pathwy

---

## Known Limitations

- **No MBE/DBE certification yet** — several high-value programs (MBDA grants, DBE set-asides) require Minority Business Enterprise certification not yet obtained. These are YELLOW status until certification is issued.
- **USPS RPDC activation pending** — USPS logistics subcontract pursuit requires PS Form 5436 filing. If not yet filed, the mailing list registration step must be completed before Step 3 of this SOP produces results.
- **Matching funds constraint** — grant programs requiring matching funds above current operating budget are LOW PRIORITY. This constrains the total addressable grant pool until capital position improves.
- **Narrative must be tailored per application** — the Community Sovereignty Narrative framework is the structure, not the final text. Each application requires 30–60 minutes of customization to the specific grant's community goals.
- **No automated grant alert system** — scouting is manual via Perplexity. Active programs may be missed between scout cycles.
- **USPS scope requires NAICS 484110** — the RPDC logistics subcontract track uses a different NAICS (General Freight Trucking) than the primary bidding SOP (561720/562111). Confirm NAICS 484110 is registered on SAM.gov before pursuing USPS opportunities.

## Failure Modes

| Scenario | What Breaks | Mitigation |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| Identity checklist items fail | Application immediately disqualified | Never skip Step 1/2 — all 7 identity items must be confirmed before submitting |
| Legal pages offline at submission | Digital Presence compliance package incomplete | Verify all three pages load before running any grant cycle |
| Generic boilerplate narrative submitted | Narrative scored poorly; application deprioritized | Always use Community Sovereignty framework — Vallejo specifics are the competitive differentiator |
| Follow-up deadline missed | Grant awarded without follow-through | Every submission logged with follow-up date in `logs/grant_submissions.md` |
| NAICS 484110 not on SAM.gov | USPS logistics bids disqualified | Add 484110 to SAM.gov entity profile before running Prompt 3 |

## Deprecation Risk

**Low for SOP logic.** Grant program landscapes change annually, but the 8-step qualification and submission sequence is stable. **Medium for specific program targets:** CalRecycle, MBDA, EPA Region 9 programs change funding cycles — verify program is still active before each application.
