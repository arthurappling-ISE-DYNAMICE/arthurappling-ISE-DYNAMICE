- **DO NOT** begin Step 1 without deposit confirmed — no work before payment

- **DO NOT** mark any step complete without the named deliverable in the client's GitHub repo
- **DO NOT** attempt Step 15 Handoff before Steps 1–14 are verified complete via final Operational Audit
- **DO NOT** accept verbal confirmation for any step — written confirmation (email is sufficient) is mandatory
