# WORKFLOW: Government Contracting & Capital Acquisition Intelligence Engine

**Objective:** Build a long-term intelligence and operational system for government contracting and capital acquisition.
**Architect:** Arthur F. Appling Sr., Prime Pathwy

## 1. Intelligence Structure Setup
Build a permanent tracking structure for procurement targets:
- California procurement and Northern California municipalities
- Federal procurement (SAM.gov, SBA programs)
- CalOSBA, BAAQMD, public purchase portals (PlanetBids, OpenGov)
- County procurement systems

Generate tracking assets:
- Tracking spreadsheets and contact databases
- RFP trackers and bid scoring systems
- Contract qualification and opportunity ranking systems

## 2. Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)
Create rigorous SOPs for the entire contracting lifecycle:
- Responding to bids and evaluating contracts
- Pricing contracts and compliance review
- Insurance verification and vendor registration

## 3. AI-Assisted Workflows
Design automated workflows to leverage AI capabilities:
- Summarizing RFPs and extracting requirements
- Generating proposal drafts
- Compliance analysis and risk scoring

## 4. Institutional Assets & Expansion
Build a searchable intelligence vault containing:
- Institutional bid language libraries
- Proposal and compliance phrase banks
- Operational templates

Include strategies for growth:
- Recurring revenue models and expansion strategies
- Subcontracting strategies and relationship tracking
- Vendor networking systems

**Validation Contract:**
- Exact Command: Command to execute the AI RFP summary script.
- Pass Criteria: A structured JSON or Markdown summary of the RFP requirements.
- Error Map: If API fails, check environment variables and retry logic.
