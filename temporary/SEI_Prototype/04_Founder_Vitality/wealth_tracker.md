## SECTION 2 — SURPLUS FLAG PROTOCOL

> When Net Surplus in any pay period exceeds $0.00 —
> the Concrete Rule activates automatically.
> The following steps execute in order. No step is optional.

**STEP 1 — FLAG THE SURPLUS**
Within 24 hours of the pay period closing, calculate the Net Surplus.
Log it in the bi-weekly ledger above. Flag the row.

**STEP 2 — CLASSIFY THE DEPLOYMENT**
Consult the Asset Priority Ladder (Section 3).
Determine which asset class receives this period's surplus.
The ladder is not a suggestion — it is a queue.

**STEP 3 — LOG THE DEPLOYMENT INTENTION**
Before moving any money, write one line in the Concrete and Steel Deployment Log (Section 4):
> "[Date] — Deploying $[amount] to [asset class] — [specific asset] — [account/wallet]"

**STEP 4 — EXECUTE WITHIN 72 HOURS**
Surplus not deployed within 72 hours of flagging must be logged as a Hold
with a written reason. Holds longer than 7 days require a Principal override entry.
Money sitting in checking is not protected. It is not deployed. It is drifting.

**STEP 5 — CONFIRM AND CLOSE**
Once deployed, mark the row ✅ in the Flag column.
Update the Asset Portfolio (Section 5) with the new position.
