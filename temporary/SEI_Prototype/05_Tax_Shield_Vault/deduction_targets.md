## CATEGORY 7 — BUSINESS MEALS · STRATEGY SESSIONS
### IRC §274 · 50% Deduction on Qualifying Business Meals

**Legal Standard:** Meals are 50% deductible when directly related to the
active conduct of business. Documentation required: date, amount, business purpose,
names of people present, and business relationship.

**Qualifying Scenarios:**

| Scenario | Deductibility | Documentation |
|----------|--------------|--------------|
| Prospect discovery call follow-up lunch | 50% | Receipt + name + business purpose |
| Client onboarding kickoff meal | 50% | Receipt + client name + install reference |
| Accountant / CPA strategy meeting | 50% | Receipt + advisor name |
| Strategic planning sessions (solo) | NOT deductible | Do not claim |

**Annual Budget:** $400–$800 in qualifying meals · Deduction: $200–$400
**Rule:** Never claim a meal without a same-day written note of business purpose.
A credit card statement alone is insufficient documentation under audit.
