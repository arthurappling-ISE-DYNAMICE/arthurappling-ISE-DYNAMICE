Prime Pathwy  
Acquisition Opportunity Appendix  
Solano County, CA  
Date: June 2024  

---

### Objective  
Identify strategic acquisition targets and/or partnership candidates within Solano County, CA, aligned with Prime Pathwy’s growth objectives in technology integration, infrastructure services, and digital transformation sectors.

---

### Market Overview  
Solano County presents a diverse business ecosystem with strengths in advanced manufacturing, logistics, clean energy, healthcare technology, and IT services. The county’s proximity to the Bay Area offers access to skilled talent and innovation hubs, balanced with comparatively lower operating costs.

---

### Criteria for Target Identification  
- **Revenue:** $3M to $15M annually  
- **EBITDA Margin:** 10%+  
- **Employee Count:** 15-75  
- **Technology Alignment:** Companies with expertise in cloud services, cybersecurity, systems integration, or IoT  
- **Customer Base:** SMBs and mid-market enterprises within healthcare, logistics, manufacturing, or government sectors  
- **Growth Potential:** Demonstrated organic growth or scalable service models  
- **Ownership:** Preference for owner-operated or private equity-backed entities open to acquisition or partnership  

---

### Target Companies & Strategic Partners  

1. **Solano IT Solutions, Inc.**  
   - *Profile:* Local IT managed services provider specializing in SMB cloud migration and cybersecurity solutions.  
   - *Financials:* Estimated $7M revenue, 12% EBITDA margin.  
   - *Strategic Fit:* Enhances Prime Pathwy’s cybersecurity and cloud offerings; established customer base in healthcare and manufacturing sectors.  
   - *Action:* Initiate exploratory discussions for acquisition or strategic alliance.

2. **BayTech Manufacturing Systems**  
   - *Profile:* Systems integrator focused on automation solutions for advanced manufacturing in Solano County’s industrial parks.  
   - *Financials:* Estimated $10M revenue, 15% EBITDA margin.  
   - *Strategic Fit:* Complementary technology integration capabilities; potential to expand Prime Pathwy’s footprint into manufacturing automation.  
   - *Action:* Conduct due diligence on technology stack and client contracts; assess acquisition viability.

3. **GreenGrid Energy Services**  
   - *Profile:* Provider of IoT-enabled energy management systems for commercial and municipal clients in Solano and adjacent counties.  
   - *Financials:* Estimated $5M revenue, 11% EBITDA margin; high growth trajectory.  
   - *Strategic Fit:* Aligns with sustainability initiatives and adds IoT expertise.  
   - *Action:* Explore partnership for co-development of energy management solutions.

4. **Valley Logistics Tech**  
   - *Profile:* Software development firm specializing in logistics tracking and supply chain optimization software.  
   - *Financials:* $4M revenue, 13% EBITDA margin; primarily serves Solano County-based logistics firms.  
   - *Strategic Fit:* Enhances Prime Pathwy’s service offerings to the logistics sector with proprietary software.  
   - *Action:* Pursue strategic partnership with option to acquire based on initial collaboration outcomes.

5. **MedSys Integration Group**  
   - *Profile:* Healthcare IT consultancy and systems integrator with a focus on EHR and compliance solutions.  
   - *Financials:* $8M revenue, 10% EBITDA margin; strong references in Solano County hospitals and clinics.  
   - *Strategic Fit:* Expands Prime Pathwy’s healthcare vertical expertise and client base.  
   - *Action:* Initiate confidential inquiry regarding acquisition interest.

---

### Recommendations  
- Prioritize initial outreach to Solano IT Solutions, Inc. and BayTech Manufacturing Systems due to immediate strategic alignment and financial stability.  
- Develop a phased engagement plan beginning with exploratory discussions, followed by technical and financial due diligence.  
- Leverage local business networks, chambers of commerce, and industry events in Solano County to validate target profiles and uncover additional opportunities.  
- Assess integration risks and cultural fit early to ensure smooth transitions post-acquisition or partnership formation.

---

### Next Steps  
- Assign dedicated acquisition team to initiate contact with target companies by Q3 2024.  
- Prepare tailored value propositions highlighting Prime Pathwy’s capabilities and potential synergies.  
- Monitor financial performance and market conditions in Solano County quarterly to adjust target list as needed.
