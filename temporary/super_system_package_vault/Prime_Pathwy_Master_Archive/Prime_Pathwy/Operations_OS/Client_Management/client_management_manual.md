# Master Operating Manual  
## Section 3: Client Management  

---

### Overview  
Client Management is a critical domain ensuring seamless engagement, clarity of expectations, and sustained partnership value. This section codifies standardized workflows and systems for onboarding, proposal generation, contract administration, and retention strategies tailored for Sovereign System installations ($5,000+).

---

### 3.1 Client Onboarding  

#### 3.1.1 Objective  
Establish a rigorous, transparent, and scalable onboarding process that sets operational expectations, validates requirements, and initiates project momentum.

#### 3.1.2 Workflow  

| Step | Action | Responsible | Deliverable | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Initial Qualification Call | Sales Lead / Pre-Sales Engineer | Qualification Checklist Completed | Validate budget, timeline, and system fit |
| 2 | Discovery Session | Technical Architect | Detailed Requirements Document | Capture environment, integrations, constraints |
| 3 | Onboarding Packet Delivery | Project Manager | Onboarding Packet (Welcome Letter, Contacts, Timeline) | Packet formatted in branded PDF, Matte Black/Gold |
| 4 | System Access Setup | IT Specialist | Client Portal Credentials | Portal enables status tracking and communication |
| 5 | Kickoff Meeting | Project Manager & Technical Architect | Meeting Minutes with Action Items | Set communication cadence, escalation paths |

#### 3.1.3 Tools & Templates  
- Onboarding Packet Template (Matte Black #0B0B0B background, Gold #C9A646 headers)  
- Qualification Checklist (Digital form)  
- Client Portal (Secure, role-based access)  

---

### 3.2 Proposal Systems  

#### 3.2.1 Objective  
Deliver consistent, transparent proposals aligned with client needs and internal pricing governance, minimizing assumptions.

#### 3.2.2 Proposal Generation Workflow  

| Step | Action | Responsible | Deliverable | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Requirement Consolidation | Technical Architect | Finalized Scope Document | Input from Discovery Session |
| 2 | Costing & Pricing | Finance/PMO | Pricing Sheet | Includes hardware, software, labor, contingency |
| 3 | Draft Proposal Creation | Proposal Specialist | Proposal Draft (branded PDF) | Matte Black/Gold theme, modular sections |
| 4 | Internal Review | Lead Architect & Finance | Reviewed Proposal | Compliance with pricing and scope |
| 5 | Client Presentation | Sales Lead | Proposal Presentation Deck | Clear system benefits, timelines, terms |
| 6 | Proposal Revision (if needed) | Proposal Specialist | Final Proposal Document | Version-controlled archive |

#### 3.2.3 Proposal Components  
- Executive Summary  
- System Architecture Overview  
- Detailed Scope & Deliverables  
- Pricing Breakdown  
- Terms & Conditions (see Contract Workflows)  

---

### 3.3 Contract Workflows  

#### 3.3.1 Objective  
Ensure compliant, clear, and enforceable contracts that define the scope, obligations, and protections for both parties.

#### 3.3.2 Contract Lifecycle  

| Phase | Action | Responsible | Deliverable | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Drafting | Generate contract from standard template | Legal / PMO | Draft Contract Document | Base on approved proposal scope |
| Review | Internal legal and architecture review | Legal, Lead Architect | Reviewed Contract | Ensure alignment with technical scope |
| Negotiation | Client discussions and amendments | Sales Lead / Legal | Redlined Contract | Track changes in version control system |
| Execution | Contract signing | Client & Authorized Signatory | Executed Contract | Digital signature preferred |
| Archival | Store contract securely | PMO / Records | Contract Archive Entry | Indexed by client ID and project number |

#### 3.3.3 Contract Template Features  
- Scope of Work  
- Fees and Payment Schedule  
- Acceptance Criteria  
- Confidentiality & IP Rights  
- Termination Clauses  
- Support & Maintenance Terms  

---

### 3.4 Retention Systems  

#### 3.4.1 Objective  
Maintain long-term client relationships through proactive engagement, performance transparency, and value reinforcement.

#### 3.4.2 Retention Framework  

| Activity | Frequency | Responsible | Description | Metrics |
|----------|-----------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| Performance Review Meetings | Quarterly | Account Manager / Technical Architect | Review system performance, usage, issues | Client satisfaction score, uptime % |
| Service Health Reports | Monthly | Support Team | Automated report on system status and alerts | Report delivery rate, issue resolution time |
| Feedback Collection | Post-Milestone & Annual | Client Success Team | Structured surveys and interviews | NPS, qualitative feedback |
| Renewal Outreach | 90 Days Before Contract End | Sales Lead | Proposal for renewal or upsell | Renewal rate, upsell success rate |
| Training & Enablement | Bi-Annual | Training Coordinator | Client workshops and knowledge transfer | Attendance, skill assessment |

#### 3.4.3 Tools & Templates  
- Automated Reporting Dashboard (Matte Black/Gold UI)  
- Client Satisfaction Survey Template  
- Renewal Proposal Playbook
