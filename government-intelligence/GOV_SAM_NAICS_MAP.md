## SAM.GOV ANNUAL RENEWAL PROTOCOL

**Renewal Window:** SAM.gov registrations expire exactly 365 days from activation/last renewal.
**Consequence of Lapse:** Any contract award during a lapsed registration is void. Any bid submitted during a lapsed registration is disqualified.
**Recommended Protocol:**
- Set a calendar alert 60 days before expiration.
- Set a second alert 30 days before expiration.
- Complete renewal no later than 14 days before expiration to allow processing time.
- Confirm renewal by checking the "Active" status badge on the SAM.gov entity page.

## DSBS PROFILE INTELLIGENCE

The Dynamic Small Business Search (DSBS) is the database contracting officers use to find small businesses. It is separate from SAM.gov but linked to it. A complete DSBS profile dramatically increases the probability of being contacted for sole-source opportunities and market research.

**Key DSBS Fields to Maximize:**
- Business description (use keywords matching your NAICS descriptions)
- Bonding levels (even if currently $0, update as capacity grows)
- EDI capability (Electronic Data Interchange — mark "Yes" if applicable)
- Government experience (list any past government work, even subcontracting)
- Keywords (add all service-related terms: janitorial, hauling, painting, debris removal, dispatch, logistics)
