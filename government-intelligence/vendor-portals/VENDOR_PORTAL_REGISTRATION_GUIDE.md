## SAM.GOV REGISTRATION — STEP BY STEP

**Pre-Requirements:**
- EIN (Federal Employer Identification Number)
- DUNS/UEI (obtain at SAM.gov)
- Business legal name and address
- NAICS codes: 561720, 561210, 562910
- Bank account for EFT payment setup
- Notarized letter (if sole proprietor)

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://sam.gov → "Get Started" → "Register Your Entity"
2. Create login.gov account (federal SSO)
3. Enter legal business name, EIN, physical address
4. Select entity type (business)
5. Enter NAICS codes
6. Complete representations and certifications (FAR/DFARS)
7. Enter banking information for EFT
8. Submit — allow 7–10 business days for activation
9. Set annual renewal reminder (registration expires annually)

**Pass Criteria:** SAM.gov dashboard shows "Active" status with UEI number displayed.

**Error Map:**
- "EIN not found" → Verify EIN with IRS; allow 2 weeks after IRS issuance
- "Address mismatch" → Use exact IRS-registered address
- "Notarized letter required" → Sole proprietors must submit notarized letter to Federal Service Desk

## CAL EPROCURE REGISTRATION — STEP BY STEP

**Pre-Requirements:**
- California business license or registration
- FEIN
- NAICS/commodity codes
- SB/DVBE certification number (if applicable)

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://www.caleprocure.ca.gov
2. Click "Register" → "New Bidder Registration"
3. Enter company profile (legal name, address, FEIN)
4. Select commodity codes (cleaning services, facilities maintenance)
5. Upload SB/DVBE certificate if applicable
6. Set notification preferences for bid alerts
7. Submit and verify email

**Pass Criteria:** Cal eProcure dashboard shows active vendor profile; bid notifications received.

## PLANETBIDS REGISTRATION — STEP BY STEP

**Note:** PlanetBids is used by Sacramento County, Sacramento City USD, Elk Grove USD, Oakland, and 100+ other agencies. One registration covers all.

**Steps:**
1. Go to https://www.planetbids.com
2. Click "Vendor Registration"
3. Enter company information
4. Select commodity codes
5. Choose agencies to follow (Sacramento County, Sacramento City USD, Elk Grove USD, Oakland USD, etc.)
6. Set email notification frequency

**Pass Criteria:** PlanetBids dashboard shows followed agencies; bid notifications active.
