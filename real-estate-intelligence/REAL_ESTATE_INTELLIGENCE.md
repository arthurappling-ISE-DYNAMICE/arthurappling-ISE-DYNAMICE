## 1. OPPORTUNITY ZONES — NORTHERN CALIFORNIA

Opportunity Zones (OZs) are federally designated census tracts offering capital gains tax deferral and elimination for long-term investments.

### Key OZ Benefits
- Defer capital gains taxes by investing in a Qualified Opportunity Fund (QOF).
- Hold for 10+ years: eliminate capital gains on OZ investment appreciation entirely.

### Northern California Opportunity Zones (Key Tracts)

|---|---|---|---|

## 2. TAX-DELINQUENT PROPERTY INTELLIGENCE

Tax-delinquent properties represent motivated sellers and distressed assets. Owners who cannot pay property taxes are often unable to maintain the property — creating immediate service opportunities.

### How to Find Tax-Delinquent Properties

| Source | URL | Data |
|---|---|---|
| Sacramento County Tax Collector | https://www.saccounty.gov/tax-collector | Delinquent tax rolls |
| Solano County Tax Collector | https://www.solanocounty.com/depts/treasurer | Delinquent tax rolls |
| Yolo County Tax Collector | https://www.yolocounty.org | Delinquent tax rolls |
| Alameda County Tax Collector | https://www.acgov.org/treasurer | Delinquent tax rolls |
| PropStream | https://propstream.com | Aggregated delinquent lists | Paid |
| ATTOM Data | https://www.attomdata.com | Delinquent + distress data | Paid |

### Action Protocol
1. Pull delinquent tax list from county (typically public record).
2. Cross-reference with property type (commercial, multi-family, industrial).
3. Skip-trace owner contact information.
4. Direct mail campaign: "We buy properties in any condition" or "We provide cleanup services for distressed properties."

- Research properties before auction: drive-by inspection, title search, environmental search.
- Target properties with cleanup/remediation needs — bid discount compensates for cleanup cost.
- Post-acquisition: Prime Pathwy provides cleanup services (internal revenue).

## 6. ABSENTEE OWNER INTELLIGENCE

Absentee owners (owners who do not live at the property) are more likely to need third-party maintenance services and are more motivated to sell.

### How to Identify Absentee Owners

| Method | Source | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Tax Bill Mailing Address | County Assessor | If tax bill goes to different address than property, owner is absentee |
| PropStream | https://propstream.com | Pre-built absentee owner lists | Paid |
| ATTOM Data | https://www.attomdata.com | Absentee owner data | Paid |
| ListSource | https://www.listsource.com | Absentee owner mailing lists | Paid |
| County Assessor Direct | Per county | Free; requires manual research |

### Absentee Owner Outreach Strategy

1. Pull absentee owner list for commercial/multi-family properties in target zip codes.
2. Segment by property type and estimated value.
3. Direct mail campaign: "We manage and maintain properties for out-of-area owners."
4. Follow up with phone call 7–10 days after mail.
5. Offer: Property maintenance agreement (monthly recurring) or one-time cleanup.
