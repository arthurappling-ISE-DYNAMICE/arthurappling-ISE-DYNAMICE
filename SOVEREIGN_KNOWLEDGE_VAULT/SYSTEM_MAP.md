## DATA FLOW ARCHITECTURE

1. **Intake:** Market data (bids, properties, competitors) flows into the **Intelligence Acquisition** nodes.
2. **Analysis:** Data is processed through the **Execution & Automation** nodes using AI Workflows.
3. **Validation:** Processed data is checked against the **Compliance & Risk** node.
4. **Funding:** Approved projects are modeled through the **Capital Allocation** node.
5. **Storage:** All final intelligence, SOPs, and system updates are permanently stored in the **Sovereign Knowledge Vault**.
