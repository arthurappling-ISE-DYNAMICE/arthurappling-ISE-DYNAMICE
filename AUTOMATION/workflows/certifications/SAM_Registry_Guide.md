# Guide 3: SAM.gov Federal Entity Registration

## I. Overview
* **Purpose:** Establish an active Unique Entity ID (UEI) to target federal set-aside contracts.
* **NAICS Codes:** 561720 (Janitorial), 562111 (Solid Waste Collection/Hauling).

## II. Execution Steps
1. **Access SAM.gov:** Log in via Login.gov using your Two-Factor Authentication.
2. **Business Info:** Enter legal business name (AA Capital INC.) and business TIN.
3. **Confirm UEI:** Record the 12-character alphanumeric code.
4. **Select NAICS:** Primary 561720, Secondary 562111.
5. **Financial Data:** Enter gross receipts and confirm small business status.
6. **Submit:** Electronically certify and sign the profile.

## III. Ongoing Maintenance
* Review quarterly and renew annually.
