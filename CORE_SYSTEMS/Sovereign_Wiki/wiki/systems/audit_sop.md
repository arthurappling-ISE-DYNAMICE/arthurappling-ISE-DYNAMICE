## PURPOSE

infrastructure review with NEPQ-driven discovery to surface pain, build urgency,
and position the Sovereign solution.

2. **PLAN:** Cross-reference findings with the [[11-Folder Master Hierarchy]].

## NEPQ DISCOVERY FRAMEWORK
*Derived from Market Gap Intelligence Report — Manus Extraction (2026-05-10)*
*Maps to 7 Sovereign Differentiation Zones: Automated Bidding · Photo Portal · Rapid Turnover · Documentation Package · Gov Bid · Eco Tier · Bundle*

### Phase 1 — Situation Questions (Open the conversation)
*Goal: Map current state before surfacing pain. Listen for vendor fragmentation, manual processes, and documentation gaps.*

1. "Walk me through how you're currently handling property turnovers when a tenant vacates."
2. "How are you getting quotes right now — phone, email, walk-throughs?"
3. "What's your current process for documenting unit condition before and after a service?"
4. "How many vendors are you managing for cleaning, hauling, and documentation separately?"
5. "Are you currently registered on SAM.gov or Cal eProcure, or pursuing any government janitorial contracts?"

### Phase 2 — Problem Awareness Questions (Surface the pain)
*Goal: Surface the specific operational pain created by each market gap. Let them say it — don't tell them.*

1. "What happens when there's a security deposit dispute and you don't have timestamped, dated photos to back you up?"
2. "How long does it typically take you to get a unit market-ready after a tenant leaves — and what do you lose while it sits?"
3. "Have you ever had a job where the quote came in slower than you needed, and you missed a window?"
4. "When you're coordinating two or three separate vendors for one turnover, where does that process usually break down?"
5. "What's the biggest frustration your property owners bring to you about how turnovers are handled right now?"

### Phase 3 — Impact Questions (Quantify the cost)
*Goal: Anchor the dollar cost of inaction. Industry data: $58–$83/day in lost rent per vacant unit at $1,740–$2,500/month.*

1. "At your current rent rates, what does every extra day your unit sits vacant actually cost you — have you ever run that number?"
2. "How many deposit disputes have cost you money this year because you couldn't document the unit's condition with hard evidence?"
3. "What does managing two separate vendor relationships — cleaning and hauling — actually cost in your own coordinator time each month?"
4. "If a government contract in the $50K–$100K range came up in Solano County right now, could you bid on it today — what's standing in the way?"
5. "If you're turning 20 units a year and each one sits 3–5 extra days, what does that add up to in lost revenue over 12 months?"

### Phase 4 — Need-Payoff Questions (Connect to solution)
*Goal: Let the prospect articulate the value of the solution in their own words before you price it.*

1. "If you had a portal that automatically timestamped before/after photos and stored them permanently for every service — how would that change your deposit defense process?"
2. "If we guaranteed your unit was market-ready within 24–48 hours of a tenant vacating, what would that be worth to you in recovered rent revenue per year?"
3. "If cleaning and hauling were bundled under one contract with digital sign-off — one vendor, one call — how much coordinator time would that recover each month?"
4. "If you could submit a government bid today and have a shot at a $100K+ annual contract, what would that do for your operation?"
5. "If your property owners could log into a portal and see real-time photo documentation for every turnover — what would that do for their confidence in you?"

### Closing Hook (Non-negotiable — spoken verbatim at end of every audit report delivery)
> "Is this the level you're ready to step into?"

## EXECUTION CHECKLIST

### Pre-Audit
- [ ] Confirm DSCR anchor: 7.42x (loan qualification reference for financial clients)
- [ ] Review client profile against Prospect Database (`../people/prospect_database.md`)
- [ ] Cross-reference target market against Market Intelligence (`../concepts/market_intelligence.md`)

### Phase 1 — SEARCH
- [ ] Run full directory tree scan (maxdepth 4, exclude node_modules / .git)
- [ ] Flag all `.lnk` files at root level
- [ ] Identify duplicate filenames across two or more directories
- [ ] Identify "unhoused" logic (`.md`, `.js`, `.py` files at root with no parent folder)
- [ ] Identify scattered image/media assets
- [ ] Note missing financial document folders (contracts, invoices)
- [ ] Record Operational Leakage Score: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH

### Phase 2 — PLAN
- [ ] Map findings to 11-Folder Master Hierarchy
- [ ] Identify top 3 structural gaps
- [ ] Draft remediation sequence (non-destructive, SEARCH-only constraints honored)

### Phase 3 — REPORT
- [ ] Generate Operational Organization Report (plain language, no jargon)
- [ ] Attach Operational Leakage Score with evidence
- [ ] Include remediation roadmap with timeline
- [ ] Deliver NEPQ discovery sequence (Phases 1–4 above)
- [ ] Close with: *"Is this the level you're ready to step into?"*
