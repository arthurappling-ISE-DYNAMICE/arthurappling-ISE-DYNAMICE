## Purpose

8-step Standard Operating Procedure for identifying, qualifying, and applying for California State and Federal Small Business grants — including USPS logistics subcontracts. Positions Prime Pathwy as a Sovereign System Integration provider operating in an underserved community (Vallejo, CA), not as a labor vendor.

Non-dilutive capital target: grants and logistics subcontracts that do not require equity, repayment, or 501(c)(3) status.
