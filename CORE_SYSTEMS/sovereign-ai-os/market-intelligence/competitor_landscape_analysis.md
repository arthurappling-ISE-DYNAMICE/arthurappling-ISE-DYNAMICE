## COMPETITIVE POSITIONING OVERVIEW

The Northern California market for janitorial, waste hauling, debris removal, facility maintenance, and commercial property turnover services is fragmented across three tiers: national franchises, regional operators, and local owner-operators. Prime Pathwy's optimal positioning is in the **local owner-operator tier** with government contract capabilities that most local operators lack.
