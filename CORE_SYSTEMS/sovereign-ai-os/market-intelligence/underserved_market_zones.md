## ZONE SCORING METHODOLOGY

Each zone is scored on five factors:
- **Demand Density** (number of potential clients per square mile)
- **Competition Level** (lower = better opportunity)
- **Government Contract Potential** (proximity to county procurement)
- **Disaster Risk** (wildfire/flood/storm exposure = disaster cleanup opportunity)
- **Demographic Alignment** (underserved communities = grant eligibility for clients)

Score: 1–5 per factor. Maximum score: 25.
