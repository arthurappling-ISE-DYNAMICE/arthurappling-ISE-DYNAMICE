## CORE SOPs

### 1. Government Procurement (`government_bid_SOP.md`)
**Purpose:** Dictates the step-by-step process for identifying, analyzing, and responding to government solicitations (RFPs, IFBs, RFQs).
**Execution Standard:** Zero-inference. All bids must be backed by documented capabilities and priced according to the Master Execution Ledger.
**Integration:** Relies on the `bid_architect.md` agent for drafting responses.

### 2. Grant Acquisition (`grant_acquisition_SOP.md`)
**Purpose:** Outlines the strategy for securing non-dilutive funding via state and federal grants (e.g., CalRecycle, EPA).
**Execution Standard:** Focuses on subcontractor positioning. Prime Pathwy partners with eligible entities (counties, nonprofits) rather than applying directly where ineligible.
**Integration:** Feeds data into the `/grants` intelligence layer.

### 3. Elite 10 Consulting (`ELITE_10_CONSULTING_FRAMEWORK.md`)
**Purpose:** The framework for deploying $5,000+ "Sovereign System" installations for high-ticket clients.
**Execution Standard:** Institutional grade. Aesthetic must remain Matte Black and Gold. Focus on Systems over Labor.
**Integration:** Utilizes the `/tools/consulting_wing` for marketing asset generation.

### 4. Client Intake (`client_intake_script.md`)
**Purpose:** Scripted methodology for qualifying and closing B2B leads.
**Execution Standard:** NEPQ-style persuasion. Focuses on uncovering deep operational pain points before presenting the Sovereign System solution.
**Integration:** Supported by the `nepq_drafter.js` tool.

### 5. System Integrity Audit (`recursive_integrity_audit.md`)
**Purpose:** A weekly protocol to ensure all files, data, and workflows adhere to the WAT Framework and Prime Pathwy standards.
**Execution Standard:** If a task fails twice, STOP. Request a Ground Truth Audit. No verbal/informal execution.
**Integration:** Driven by the `ooda_orchestrator.py` tool.
