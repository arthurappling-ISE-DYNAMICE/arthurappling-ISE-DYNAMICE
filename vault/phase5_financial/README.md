## 2. CAPITAL ALLOCATION & CASH-FLOW SYSTEMS

Sovereign financial management requires strict capital allocation rules to ensure the business remains highly liquid, audit-ready, and capable of weathering economic downturns.

### The 10-30-60 Cash Allocation Rule

All incoming client revenue must be automatically distributed across dedicated bank accounts according to strict percentage allocations:

- **10% - Operating Reserves:** Transferred to an isolated, high-yield savings account until 6 months of fixed operating expenses are secured.
- **30% - Tax Reserve:** Transferred to a dedicated tax account to cover quarterly estimated federal and state liabilities.
- **60% - Operational Capital:** Retained in the primary operating account to cover payroll, software subscriptions, marketing, and immediate growth initiatives.

### Corporate Bank Account Architecture

```
                       [ INCOMING CLIENT REVENUE ]
                                    │
                                    ▼
                        [ PRIMARY CLEARING ACCT ]
                                    │
         ┌──────────────────────────┼──────────────────────────┐
         ▼ (10%)                    ▼ (30%)                    ▼ (60%)
  [ RESERVE ACCOUNT ]        [ TAX RESERVE ACCT ]       [ OPERATING ACCOUNT ]
  (High-Yield Savings)       (Quarterly Tax Payments)   (Payroll, SaaS, Marketing)
```

## 3. ACCOUNTING & AUDIT-READINESS SYSTEMS

To defend against tax audits and support chargeback defense, the enterprise must maintain institutional-grade bookkeeping standards.

### Bookkeeping Standards

- **Double-Entry Accrual Accounting:** All financial transactions must be recorded using double-entry accrual accounting methods via localized ledger tools or enterprise platforms.
- **Weekly Reconciliation:** All bank statements must be reconciled against the general ledger every Friday by 17:00.
- **Receipt Archiving:** Every transaction over $25.00 must have a matching digital receipt attached to the ledger entry. Receipts must be stored in a structured directory: `/mnt/storage/financial/receipts/YYYY/MM/`.

### Financial Audit Checklist

- [ ] Verify all bank accounts match the general ledger balances exactly.
- [ ] Ensure all 1099 contractor agreements and W-9 forms are signed and archived.
- [ ] Confirm all quarterly estimated tax payments have been made and logged.
- [ ] Audit all corporate card expenses to ensure zero personal-use commingling.
- [ ] Generate and review the Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss (P&L), and Cash Flow Statements.

Leveraging debt responsibly allows the enterprise to accelerate growth without diluting equity ownership.
