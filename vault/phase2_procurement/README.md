## 1. GOVERNMENT & MUNICIPAL PROCUREMENT SYSTEMS

Government procurement represents a massive, highly structured, and recurring revenue stream. Understanding how federal, state, and municipal entities buy services is critical to capturing $5,000+ "Sovereign System" installations.

### Procurement Framework Comparison

| Metric | Federal Procurement (SAM.gov) | State/Municipal Procurement | Commercial Enterprise B2B |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Contract Cycle** | 6 to 18 Months | 3 to 9 Months | 1 to 3 Months |
| **Typical Value** | $100,000 - $10,000,000+ | $25,000 - $1,000,000+ | $5,000 - $250,000+ |
| **Barrier to Entry** | High (UEI, CAGE, NIST 800-171) | Medium (State Registration, COI) | Low (Mutual NDA, MSA) |
| **Payment Terms** | Net 30 (Prompt Payment Act) | Net 30 to Net 60 | Net 15 to Net 30 |
| **Regulatory Burden** | High (FAR/DFARS compliance) | Medium (Local ordinances, PLA) | Low (Contractual terms only) |

### Key Federal Procurement Identifiers
- **Unique Entity ID (UEI):** The primary identifier required to do business with the US Federal Government.
- **CAGE Code (Commercial and Government Entity):** A five-character ID assigned to support procurement and logistics.
- **NAICS Codes (North American Industry Classification System):** Standardized codes used to classify business establishments (e.g., `541511` for Custom Computer Programming Services).

## 3. COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE & FACILITIES MANAGEMENT

Commercial real estate (CRE) and facilities management operate on long-term, high-value recurring contracts. These industries suffer from significant technology fragmentation.

### CRE Service Inefficiencies

- **Vendor Fragmentation:** A single commercial building often employs dozens of disconnected subcontractors for HVAC, janitorial, landscaping, and security services.
- **Paper-Based Maintenance Logs:** Compliance and maintenance logs are frequently kept in physical binders, preventing real-time auditability and leading to expensive regulatory fines.
- **Unoptimized Routing:** Field technicians spend up to 30% of their billable hours driving due to unoptimized dispatching and scheduling systems.

### Sovereign System Solutions for CRE

- **Unified Operations Dashboard:** Integrates all subcontractor KPIs and schedules into a single, real-time command center for property managers.
- **AI-Powered Dispatch Engine:** Dynamically routes field service technicians based on real-time traffic, technician skill level, and job priority.
- **Automated Compliance Archiving:** Automatically extracts maintenance logs from subcontractor emails and indexes them into an audit-ready digital vault.
