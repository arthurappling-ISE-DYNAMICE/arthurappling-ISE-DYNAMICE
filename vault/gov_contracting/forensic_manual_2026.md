## 1. Verified 2026 Regulatory Mandate Matrix
- **CARB Clean Truck Check (13 CCR §§ 2195-2199.1):** semi-annual testing deadlines determined by DMV registration expiration. Inflation-adjusted compliance fee is $32.13 per vehicle. Failing windows risks $10,000/day daily fines and immediate registration blocks.
- **CARB Advanced Clean Fleets (ACF State & Government Fleet Portion):** Municipal waste fleets must maintain 50% ZEV or Near-ZEV purchase limits through the end of 2026, rising to 100% on Jan 1, 2027.
- **ACF Private & Drayage Fleet Portion (REPEALED):** Officially repealed by CARB vote on September 25, 2025.
- **Interstate Pass-Through Rule:** Despite U.S. EPA action on 40 CFR § 52.249, CARB enforcement directive dated March 10, 2026 mandates out-of-state vehicles must report, pay, and test to avoid roadside CHP citations.

## 2. Unclaimed Capital Stacking Rules
- **HVIP Base Refuse Vouchers:** Class 4-5 ($75k) \| Class 6-7 ($106,250) \| Class 8 ($150,000).
- **HVIP Small Business Refuse Vouchers:** Private fleets with less than 100 vehicles and less than $15M revenue get enhanced caps: Class 4-5 ($130k) \| Class 6-7 ($160k) \| Class 8 ($330k). Lifetime cap of 5 enhanced vouchers per fleet.
- **Commercial California Clean Fuel Reward (CCFR):** Re-launched with $1 Billion through 2030. Point-of-sale rebates: Class 5 ($60k) \| Class 6-7 ($85k) \| Class 8 ($120k).
- **Solano Workforce Stability Grant Tiers:** Small businesses (<500 employees) with at least 1 employee:
  - Anheuser-Busch Closure Corridor: Up to $10,000.
  - Mare Island Dry Dock Corridor: Up to $10,000.
  - Valero Refinery Closure Corridor: Up to $25,000.
