## NEW PROJECT ONBOARDING CHECKLIST

- [ ] Review insurance requirements in RFP against current policies.

### 2. Insurance & Bonding
- [ ] Request Certificate of Insurance (COI) from broker.
- [ ] Ensure client is listed as "Additional Insured" on the COI.
- [ ] Verify Workers' Compensation coverage is active.
- [ ] Obtain performance/payment bonds (if required by contract).

### 3. Safety & Cal/OSHA
- [ ] Assign a designated Safety Officer for the site.
- [ ] Ensure the site-specific Injury and Illness Prevention Program (IIPP) is available.
- [ ] Place a physical Safety Data Sheet (SDS) binder on-site.
- [ ] Verify all required PPE (gloves, goggles, masks) is provided to staff.
- [ ] Post required labor law posters at the job site or break area.

### 4. Labor & Payroll
- [ ] Confirm staff are paid at or above the local minimum wage.
- [ ] If prevailing wage: Determine correct craft classification and wage rate.

- [ ] Ensure timekeeping system tracks meal and rest breaks accurately.

### 5. Environmental
- [ ] Confirm disposal plan for any hazardous waste or biohazards.
