## SECTION 1 — OPERATIONAL DIRECTIVE

A sovereign one-man enterprise scales not through labor, but through deterministic systems. Every repeatable action must be documented as a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP). This creates institutional memory, ensures compliance, and allows for seamless delegation to AI agents or future human operators.

The core operational philosophy is **Zero-Inference Execution**: If a task fails twice, stop. Request a ground truth audit. Never assume.

---

## SECTION 2 — CLIENT INTAKE & ONBOARDING SYSTEM (SOP-01)

### Objective
Ensure all new clients (commercial or government) are legally bound, financially verified, and operationally integrated before work begins.

### Workflow
1. **Initial Contact:** Client expresses interest via web form, email, or inbound call.
2. **Pre-Qualification:** Automated form captures budget, timeline, and scope. Disqualify leads outside target parameters.
3. **Proposal Generation:** Generate standardized proposal using pre-approved templates. Include detailed scope of work and pricing.
4. **Contract Execution:** Client signs digital contract (e.g., DocuSign). Contract MUST include payment terms, cancellation policy, and liability waivers.
5. **Financial Verification:** Collect initial deposit or verify purchase order (PO) for government contracts. Do not schedule work without financial commitment.
6. **Operational Integration:** Create client profile in CRM/dispatch system. Assign project manager or lead technician.
7. **Welcome Sequence:** Automated email sequence outlines what to expect, communication protocols, and emergency contacts.

## SECTION 3 — INVOICE & DISPUTE RESPONSE SYSTEM (SOP-02)

### Objective
Ensure rapid payment collection and defend against chargebacks or invoice disputes with audit-ready documentation.

### Workflow
1. **Milestone Tracking:** Project manager logs completion of specific milestones in the field management app.
2. **Automated Invoicing:** System generates invoice immediately upon milestone completion. Include detailed line items and photo evidence if applicable.
3. **Payment Reminders:** Automated reminders at 3 days before due date, on due date, and 3, 7, and 14 days past due.
4. **Dispute Response Protocol:**
   - Acknowledge dispute within 24 hours.
   - Compile "Audit Packet": Signed contract, digital proof of delivery/completion (photos, signatures), communication logs.
   - Submit Audit Packet to client or payment processor.
   - If unresolved after 14 days, escalate to collections or legal counsel.

## SECTION 4 — OSHA & COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK (SOP-03)

### Objective
Maintain strict compliance with state and federal safety regulations to prevent fines, protect workers, and maintain eligibility for government contracts.

### Workflow
1. **Daily Safety Logs:** Foremen complete digital safety checklist before work begins. Must include hazard identification and mitigation steps.
2. **Incident Reporting:** Any injury or near-miss must be logged immediately. Include photos, witness statements, and corrective actions.
3. **Training Records:** Maintain digital database of all employee certifications (e.g., OSHA 10/30, CPR, specialized equipment).
4. **Annual Audit:** Conduct comprehensive review of safety protocols and update based on new regulations.

---

## SECTION 5 — EMERGENCY RESPONSE PLAYBOOK (SOP-04)

### Objective
Ensure rapid, coordinated response to critical incidents (e.g., severe injury, major property damage, natural disaster).

### Workflow
1. **Immediate Action:** Secure the scene. Call 911 if necessary. Ensure safety of all personnel.
2. **Notification:** Notify project manager, safety officer, and executive leadership immediately.
3. **Documentation:** Capture photos, videos, and witness statements. Do not admit fault or speculate on causes.
4. **Client Communication:** Inform client of the incident and initial steps taken. Maintain professional, objective tone.
5. **Post-Incident Review:** Conduct thorough investigation to determine root cause and implement corrective actions.

## SECTION 6 — AUTOMATION & AI WORKFLOW INTEGRATION

These SOPs are designed to be executed by AI agents where possible. For example, the Invoice & Dispute Response System (SOP-02) can be fully automated using tools like Zapier or Make, connecting the field management app to the accounting software and CRM. The goal is to minimize manual data entry and maximize operational leverage.
