| Threshold | Rule |
|---|---|
| Under $10,000 | Micro-purchase — no competition required |
| $10,001 – $250,000 | Simplified Acquisition Threshold — small business set-aside mandatory |
| $250,001 – $750,000 | Full and open competition OR set-aside |
