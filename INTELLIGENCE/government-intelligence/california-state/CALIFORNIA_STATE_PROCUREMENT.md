| Threshold | Rule |
|---|---|
| Under $5,000 | Informal — direct purchase, no bid required |
| $5,001 – $50,000 | Informal competitive — 3 quotes required |

## VENDOR REGISTRATION STEPS — CAL EPROCURE

1. Navigate to https://www.caleprocure.ca.gov
2. Click "Register" — create bidder profile
3. Enter business legal name, FEIN, NAICS codes
4. Upload SB/DVBE certification (if applicable)
5. Set commodity codes for bid notifications
6. Complete profile — receive email notifications for matching bids
7. Submit bids electronically through portal
