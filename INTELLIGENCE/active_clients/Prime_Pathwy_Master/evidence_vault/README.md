# EVIDENCE VAULT: AUDIT-READY VERIFICATION SYSTEM

## MISSION:
To provide 100% indisputable proof of work for milestone payments, grant compliance, and chargeback defense.

## FOLDER STRUCTURE:
1. **/Before:** Mandatory timestamped photos before any labor begins.
2. **/During:** Progress photos required for Phase 1, 2, and 3 milestone triggers.
3. **/After:** Final "Hancock" photos showing 100% completion and cleanup.
4. **/Receipts:** Digital copies of all material purchases for audit-readiness.

## VERIFICATION RULES:
- **GPS Lock:** AI must verify photo coordinates match the project site.
- **Timestamp Integrity:** Photos must be uploaded within 2 hours of the "Date Taken" metadata.
- **Resolution Standard:** All photos must be high-resolution; blurry images do not trigger payments.

## RULE ZERO:
No photo = No work = No pay.
