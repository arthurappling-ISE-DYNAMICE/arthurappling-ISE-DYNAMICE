I. The AB 2019 Legislative Framework

California's legislative environment creates a structural mandate — not a preference — for Small Business participation in state procurement. Under California Government Code §14837 et seq., state agencies are required to award 25% of total contract dollars to certified Small Businesses annually. Assembly Bill 2019 reinforced this framework by expanding agency accountability and extending SB procurement preferences into services and infrastructure contracts — the precise categories in which Prime Pathwy operates.

Strategic Implication: DGS SB Certification (application in progress) is the master access key. Upon certification, AA Capital INC is immediately eligible for the state's preferred-vendor pipeline. This is a legislatively mandated demand source — it does not contract during economic downturns.
