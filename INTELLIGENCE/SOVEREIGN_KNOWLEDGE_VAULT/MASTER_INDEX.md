## 4. THE WAT FRAMEWORK (HARD RULE)

All operational output within Prime Pathwy MUST adhere to the WAT Framework:

- **Workflows (.md) -> `/workflows`**: Human-readable or AI-parsable step-by-step processes.
- **Agents (Prompts) -> `/agents`**: AI persona definitions, system prompts, and custom instructions.
- **Tools (Scripts/Python) -> `/tools`**: Executable code, scripts, automation logic.
- **Temporary/Data -> `/temporary`**: Scratchpads, raw data dumps, and in-progress work.

*Note: The Sovereign Knowledge Vault serves as the permanent, immutable record of the WAT framework outputs once they reach production status.*
