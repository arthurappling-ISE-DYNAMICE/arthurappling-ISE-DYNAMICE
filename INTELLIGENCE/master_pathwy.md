### PHASE I — INTELLIGENCE & DIAGNOSIS
*Steps 1–3 | Timeline: Weeks 1–4*

---

#### Step 1 — Operational Autopsy
**Objective:** Map every manual process, hidden bottleneck, and single-point-of-failure in the client's operation.

- Conduct a structured 90-minute Discovery Session using NEPQ-style diagnostic questions
- Document all current workflows: dispatch, quoting, invoicing, crew communication, job tracking
- Identify every task the owner personally handles that could be delegated or automated
- Produce: **Operational Autopsy Report** — a written inventory of every process, its current state, and its dependency on human attention

**Value Delivered:** Client sees their chaos documented in writing for the first time. The visual weight of the report alone justifies the engagement.

---

#### Step 2 — Chaos Quantification Audit
**Objective:** Assign a dollar cost to every inefficiency uncovered in Step 1.

- Calculate time spent per week on each manual process
- Multiply by the owner's effective hourly rate and project annually
- Identify revenue leakage: missed follow-ups, delayed invoices, unbilled change orders
- Produce: **Chaos Cost Statement** — a financial document showing the true annual cost of operating without systems

**Value Delivered:** Converts abstract frustration into hard numbers. A client spending $80,000/year in operational chaos can clearly see the ROI of a $5,000 system investment.

---

#### Step 3 — Sovereign Blueprint Design
**Objective:** Architect the client's future-state Sovereign System before a single tool is deployed.

- Design the full operational architecture: workflows, automation triggers, handoff points, reporting layers
- Define the delegation map: what gets automated, what gets delegated, what the owner retains
- Set 90-day, 6-month, and 18-month operational milestones
- Produce: **Sovereign Blueprint** — a visual and written system architecture document the client can present to partners, investors, or lenders

**Value Delivered:** Client holds a professional system design before any implementation begins. Institutional credibility established.

---

### PHASE II — FOUNDATION & INFRASTRUCTURE
*Steps 4–7 | Timeline: Weeks 5–12*

---

#### Step 4 — Identity Sovereignty
**Objective:** Establish the client's digital infrastructure as a sovereign, professional entity.

- Register or audit professional domain and business email
- Deploy managed GitHub repository as the client's operational command record
- Establish brand standards: logo, colors, document templates aligned to their market position
- Produce: **Identity Sovereignty Package** — domain, email, GitHub repo, and brand guide

**Value Delivered:** The business now looks and operates like an institution, not a hustle.

---

#### Step 5 — Command Center Deployment
**Objective:** Stand up the client's centralized operational hub — the single source of truth for all business activity.

- Deploy project management and operations platform (configured to their industry)
- Build job pipeline views: Hauling dispatch boards, Painting project trackers, Logistics load management
- Configure team access, permissions, and notification protocols
- Produce: **Command Center** — live operational dashboard, fully configured and documented

**Value Delivered:** Owner has one place to see everything. Chaos collapses into clarity.

---

#### Step 6 — Workflow Engineering
**Objective:** Convert every manual process into a documented, repeatable Standard Operating Procedure.

- Build written SOPs for all core operations: lead intake, quoting, job scheduling, crew briefing, job completion, invoicing, follow-up
- Format all SOPs to Prime Pathwy Institutional Standard: step-by-step, decision trees included, delegation-ready
- Store all SOPs in the client's managed GitHub repository under the WAT framework
- Produce: **Operational Manual** — the client's complete documented business playbook

**Value Delivered:** For the first time, the business can run without the owner being physically present for every decision.

---

#### Step 7 — Tool Stack Deployment
**Objective:** Install and configure the automation toolkit that powers the Sovereign System.

- Select and deploy tools appropriate to the client's industry and scale (CRM, scheduling, invoicing, communication)
- Integrate tools into a connected ecosystem — no orphaned apps
- Document every tool: purpose, login credentials (secured), configuration logic, and maintenance protocol
- Produce: **Tool Stack Registry** — a complete inventory of every system deployed, how it connects, and who owns it

**Value Delivered:** Client has a professional, integrated technology stack — not a collection of unconnected subscriptions.

---

### PHASE III — AUTOMATION & SYSTEMS
*Steps 8–11 | Timeline: Months 3–9*

---

#### Step 8 — Lead Capture Sovereignty
**Objective:** Automate the entire front end of the client's revenue pipeline.

- Build or optimize lead intake forms, landing pages, and inbound routing
- Deploy CRM automations: instant acknowledgment, follow-up sequences, quote request triggers
- Configure lead scoring and prioritization rules
- Produce: **Lead Capture System** — automated front-end pipeline that qualifies and routes leads without owner involvement

**Value Delivered:** Owner stops losing revenue to slow follow-up. Leads are captured and worked automatically.

---

#### Step 9 — Dispatch & Scheduling Automation
**Objective:** Remove the owner from the daily operational dispatch loop.

- Build automated job scheduling workflows tied to crew availability and job type
- Deploy client communication automations: booking confirmations, day-before reminders, arrival notifications
- Configure crew assignment logic and exception escalation rules
- Produce: **Dispatch Sovereignty System** — automated scheduling engine that runs daily operations

**Value Delivered:** Owner is no longer the dispatcher. Crew knows where to be. Clients are informed automatically.

---

#### Step 10 — Invoice & Payment Sovereignty
**Objective:** Automate the client's entire billing and collections cycle.

- Build automated invoice generation triggered by job completion
- Deploy payment collection sequences: due-date reminders, overdue escalations, receipt confirmations
- Configure revenue reporting: weekly collections summary, outstanding balance dashboard
- Produce: **Payment Sovereignty System** — automated billing engine that collects revenue without manual follow-up

**Value Delivered:** Owner stops chasing payments. Money moves automatically.

---

#### Step 11 — Sovereign Reporting Dashboard
**Objective:** Give the owner real-time visibility into every critical business metric.

- Build KPI dashboard: revenue, jobs completed, outstanding invoices, crew utilization, lead conversion rate
- Configure weekly automated reports delivered to owner's inbox
- Establish monthly performance review protocol
- Produce: **Sovereign Dashboard** — live business intelligence layer giving the owner CEO-level visibility

**Value Delivered:** Owner makes decisions from data, not instinct. The business becomes measurable and therefore improvable.

---

### PHASE IV — OPTIMIZATION & SCALE
*Steps 12–15 | Timeline: Months 9–18*

---

#### Step 12 — Team Sovereignty Protocol
**Objective:** Build the infrastructure to hire, onboard, and delegate to team members without owner involvement.

- Create role-specific onboarding SOPs for all positions (crew lead, dispatcher, office admin)
- Build delegation frameworks: what authority each role has, escalation paths, performance expectations
- Deploy team communication and accountability systems
- Produce: **Team Sovereignty Protocol** — complete hiring, onboarding, and delegation infrastructure

**Value Delivered:** Owner can now hire and trust — the business scales without requiring their personal supervision of every new person.

---

#### Step 13 — Client Experience System
**Objective:** Automate the post-job client relationship to generate reviews, referrals, and repeat business.

- Build post-job follow-up sequences: satisfaction check, review request, referral ask
- Deploy client loyalty touchpoints: anniversary messages, seasonal promotions, re-engagement campaigns
- Configure reputation management: review monitoring and response protocols
- Produce: **Client Experience System** — automated relationship layer that generates social proof and repeat revenue

**Value Delivered:** Owner earns reviews and referrals while sleeping. Reputation compounds automatically.

---

#### Step 14 — Growth Infrastructure
**Objective:** Install the systems that turn a stabilized operation into a scaling enterprise.

- Build referral program infrastructure: partner agreements, tracking, payout protocols
- Design service expansion framework: how to add new service lines or territories using existing SOPs
- Deploy financial planning layer: revenue targets, capacity modeling, hiring triggers
- Produce: **Growth Infrastructure Package** — the strategic layer that converts operational stability into intentional expansion

**Value Delivered:** Owner now has a machine designed to grow — not just survive.

---

#### Step 15 — Sovereign Handoff
**Objective:** Transfer full ownership of the Sovereign System to the client with complete documentation, training, and independence.

- Conduct final Operational Audit: verify all 14 prior systems are functional, documented, and delegated
- Deliver completed Operational Manual, Tool Stack Registry, and Sovereign Blueprint (updated to reflect final state)
- Conduct 3-hour Handoff Session: walk owner through every system, every automation, every SOP
- Transfer all credentials, repositories, and platform ownership to client
- Produce: **Sovereign Handoff Package** — complete system documentation, GitHub repo, training recording, and 30-day post-handoff support protocol

**Value Delivered:** Client achieves 100% operational freedom. They own a Sovereign System — not a dependency on Prime Pathwy. This is the mission fulfilled.
