## INDEX DIRECTORY

This index provides a semantic map of all critical intelligence assets within the Sovereign AI Operating System.

### 1. Procurement Intelligence (`/procurement`)
*   `procurement_master_registry.csv`: Master database of target government agencies, portals, and active bids.
*   `agency_relationship_map.md`: Strategic mapping of key procurement officers and SB/DVBE advocates.
*   `incumbent_vendor_analysis.md`: Profiles of existing contractors holding target government contracts.
*   `recurring_contract_cycles.csv`: Calendar of when major county/state contracts are expected to rebid.
*   `direct_award_opportunities.csv`: Guide to accessing non-competitive bids via SB/DVBE/SLEB certifications.

### 2. Grant Intelligence (`/grants`)
*   `sovereign_grant_master_database.csv`: Database of state and federal grants (CalRecycle, EPA, FEMA).
*   `strategic_funding_map.md`: The strategy for acting as a subcontractor to grant-funded public entities.
*   `grant_cycles_and_patterns.md`: Annual calendar of grant application and award windows.

### 3. Market Intelligence (`/market-intelligence`)
*   `norcal_b2b_master_leads.csv`: Database of target property management and commercial real estate firms.
*   `competitor_landscape_analysis.md`: Analysis of national franchises vs. local operators.
*   `underserved_market_zones.md`: Scoring of NorCal cities based on demand density and competition.
*   `subcontracting_opportunity_matrix.csv`: Mapping of opportunities to serve as a sub to larger prime contractors.

### 4. AI OS Infrastructure (`/ai-infrastructure`)
*   `system_map.md`: High-level overview of the repository and its sub-systems.
*   `dependency_graph.md`: Map of how workflows, agents, and tools interact.
*   `workflow_registry.csv`: Master list of all active Standard Operating Procedures.
*   `operational_sop_library.md`: Index of core execution protocols.
*   `architecture_overview.md`: Design principles of the data, logic, and agent layers.

### 5. Sports Analytics (`/sports-analytics`)
*   `nfl_quant_master_dataset.csv`: 855-game dataset (2023-2025) with spreads, totals, and weather.
*   `line_movement_analysis.csv`: Analysis of home cover rates and total line efficiency.
*   `scheduling_disadvantage_report.csv`: Impact of short rest (Thursday Night Football).
*   `weather_correlation_data.csv`: Impact of cold and wind on scoring totals.

### 6. Knowledge Management (`/knowledge-management`)
*   `lessons_learned.md`: Log of strategic insights and course corrections.
*   `architecture_decisions.md`: Record of structural decisions (ADRs).
*   `optimization_history.md`: Log of system upgrades and future targets.
*   `institutional_memory_index.md`: This file.
