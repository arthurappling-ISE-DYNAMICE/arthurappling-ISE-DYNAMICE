### 2. THE DIALOGUE SCRIPT (NEPQ COLD CALL)

**Connection Questions:**
**You:** "Hey [Owner's Name], it's Arthur. I saw your junk removal trucks operating around Napa. Are you the one fielding the incoming calls for new hauling jobs?"
**Prospect:** "Yeah, they come straight to my cell."
**You:** "Got it. I help hauling companies in Northern California capture more leads. Just curious, when you're in the middle of a heavy cleanout and can't answer the phone, are those potential clients leaving voicemails, or are they just calling the next guy on Google?"

**Problem Awareness Questions:**
**You:** "Okay, so when a potential client calls the next company because you couldn't answer, how much revenue would you say you're losing every week just from missed calls?"
**Prospect:** "Probably a few hundred bucks, maybe more."
**You:** "I see. And how does it feel knowing you're paying for marketing or working hard for referrals, only to lose the job because you were physically unable to pick up the phone?"

**Tool/Solution Awareness Questions:**
**You:** "Right. It sounds like relying on a basic mobile line is creating a leak in your business. What if your system automatically texted back any missed call within 5 seconds, asking what they need hauled and providing a link to book an estimate?"
**Prospect:** "That would save a lot of lost jobs."
**You:** "Exactly. Because right now, that legacy manual workflow is costing you direct revenue."

**Consequence Questions:**
**You:** "Let's say you keep running the business off your personal cell phone. What happens over the next 12 months as you get busier and miss even more calls?"
